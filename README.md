**Your daily health companion, designed for real life.**

Life moves fast. Symptoms, moods, energy levels, and triggers don’t have to be a mystery.

Symptom Journal gives you a clean, private space to log what’s happening and instantly see the connections that matter — like how a change in sleep, diet, stress, or routine shows up in how you feel.

Whether you’re managing a chronic condition, tracking allergies or side effects, supporting mental wellness, or simply optimizing your everyday health, this app turns scattered notes into clear, empowering insights.

---

## ✨ Features That Make a Difference

- **📝 Effortless Daily Logging** — Quick entry for symptoms with severity sliders, detailed notes, and custom tags  
- **🌟 Mood & Energy Tracking** — Simple check-ins that capture how you’re really feeling  
- **📈 Beautiful, Actionable Insights** — Victory-powered charts and trends that reveal patterns over time  
- **🔍 Pattern Discovery** — Connect the dots between food, sleep, stress, weather, medication, and your symptoms  
- **📄 Professional Reports** — One-tap PDF summaries you can print or share with your doctor  
- **🔔 Gentle Reminders** — Custom push notifications to help you stay consistent without the hassle  
- **🔒 Complete Privacy First** — Secure local storage + optional Supabase sync (your data, your rules)  
- **🌗 Calming Design** — Elegant dark and light modes that feel good to use every single day

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Framework**: Expo SDK 54 (Managed Workflow) — runs smoothly on Android  
- **Language**: TypeScript  
- **Navigation**: React Navigation (Native Stack + Bottom Tabs)  
- **Storage & Sync**:  
  - Local: `@react-native-async-storage/async-storage` + `expo-secure-store`  
  - Appwrite Backend  
- **Visualizations**: Victory Native — clean, professional charts  
- **Utilities**:  
  - Notifications: `expo-notifications`  
  - Reports & Sharing: `expo-print` + `expo-sharing`  
  - Device Info: `expo-device` + `expo-constants`  

**Core**: React 19 + React Native 0.81
